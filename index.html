    • Welcome

    Between historical and modern buildings, the touristic Downtown and its surroundings abundant of live nature, Ottawa set up urban, adventure, and fancy styles.

    The city is a scenario of art galleries, museums, government houses, fabulous restaurants, parks, rivers, and shopping malls. Full of festivals and events of enormous proportion, such as astonishing fireworks celebrations in Canada Day and New Eve; amazing gardens dressed with thousands of colored flowers in the Tulip Festival; farms with fields filled with berries, apples, and other fruits for seasoning picking up; exhibitions, ice sculptures, and the time of skating at Rideau Canal during the Winterlude Festival. It is not possible to exhaust Ottawa in a single introduction. But, as you are just arriving, I want to change the concept that Ottawa is just the capital of Canada or a place to spend only a day trip.
